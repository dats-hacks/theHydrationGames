Water Shortage

Have you ever had a sneaking suspicion that your rival in sports is also using too much water in the locker room! Gasp. Well now you can find out. Use the skills you have learned in Environmental Science to compete against fellow schools for the LOWEST WATER USAGE

Tips to Win

We have even created a nifty page of helpful tips to win this compitition. Like turning off the faucet between rinse / soap / rinse. Or not using water baloons and instead having coding compititions, and really who needs a shower after track practice?

The Rules

No cheating. No really. You must be in a public school. and just so you know when you are at a homegame of another school turning on all their faucets is considered cheating!!
